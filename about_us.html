About Us

Our Mission

At Phishing Awareness, our mission is to educate and empower people to recognize and avoid phishing attacks. We believe that everyone should be equipped with the knowledge to stay safe online, and we’re dedicated to providing accessible resources to help protect users from cyber threats.

Who We Are

We are a team of cybersecurity enthusiasts and professionals who have come together with a shared goal: to make online spaces safer. With backgrounds in cybersecurity, technology, and education, we’re committed to raising awareness about phishing and other cyber attacks that target individuals and organizations.

What We Offer

  • Educational Articles: In-depth articles that explain phishing and other common cyber threats in simple terms.
  • Infographics: Visual guides that illustrate phishing tactics and provide tips for recognizing scams.
  • Videos: Short, engaging videos that teach users how to spot phishing attempts and avoid falling victim to scams.
  • Discussion Forum: A platform for users to ask questions, share experiences, and learn from one another.

Our Values

Our values are centered on education, accessibility, and user safety. We aim to make cybersecurity knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or experience level. Our goal is to empower our audience to make informed decisions that keep them safe online.
